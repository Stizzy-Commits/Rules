Server Rules

Read these before playing. These rules cover general conduct, vehicles, raiding, bases, homes, shops, and job-specific behavior.

General Rules

13 rules

01
  • 1.1
    NLR applies here, do not return to your place of death for at least 3 minutes

    You forget everything that happened in the previous life.

  • 1.2
    Acting on and/or relaying information in OOC is metagame, this is not allowed.
  • 1.3
    Roleplay related information must be posted in /advert chat and will be considered metagaming if posted in OOC chat(s).
  • 1.4
    English only

    (You can speak any language via PM or in private areas)

  • 1.5
    Don't build on any public area

    Hobos are an exception. Radio Hosts and Medics may build a shack/stand in public.

  • 1.6
    Random Death Match (RDM) is NOT allowed.
  • 1.7
    Prop-abusing is NOT allowed.
  • 1.8
    Inappropriate or offensive prop constructions are not allowed.
  • 1.9
    You can kill players who insult you after you have given them a text warning to stop and they continue.
  • 1.10
    Do not block off public areas, roads, streets, etc.
  • 1.11
    Any sort of hacking/cheating isn't allowed.
  • 1.12
    Threatening to attack the server in any manner will result in a permanent ban.
  • 1.13
    Bug abuse of any sort is not allowed.

Car Rules

3 rules

02
  • 2.1
    If someone is on your car you may shoot them after giving them a warning.
  • 2.2
    If the driver of a vehicle is not a co-owner of the car he is driving, cops can assume they are stealing.
  • 2.3
    Do not block public places or bases with cars

    Do not attempt to drive cars into the water or inside buildings that were not made for cars.

Raiding Rules

14 rules

03
  • 3.1
    Do not minge raid

    Raiding a base you know has absolutely nothing in it or raiding an unowned base just to shoot people.

  • 3.2
    Props are not a reason to raid or kill.
  • 3.3
    If a base is open and you're able to examine it without any restrictions, you cannot raid it unless you find valuables inside the base.
  • 3.4
    The police can raid to confiscate printers, drugs and weapons but only with a valid warrant. They cannot steal legal items - Health, armor or money.
  • 3.5
    After being raided, you must wait 3 minutes before returning to your home (NLR)
  • 3.6
    You must wait 5 minutes after raiding, before raiding the same person again.
  • 3.7
    Thieves, Parkours & Gangsters(2 or more) are the only jobs allowed to raid

    Hitmen may raid to take out their hit.

  • 3.8
    Gangsters & Mob Boss together are able to raid the PD to release gang members from prison.
  • 3.9
    The only other job allowed to raid the PD are terrorists with Osama.
  • 3.10
    You CAN be raided while building

    Create an entrance by placing a keypad & fading door before building anything else!

  • 3.11
    You may kill someone in order to steal something that they are holding or if a car owner is standing next to their vehicle.
  • 3.12
    You do not need to /advert raid or mug.
  • 3.13
    If another player is in the process of lock-picking a vehicle that you desire, you may kill them in order to steal it yourself.
  • 3.14
    The Radio and Radio host's microphone are not reasons to raid

    You are not to steal the Radio host's microphone.

Shop, Bases & Home Rules

26 rules

04
  • 4.1
    You may only have THREE fading doors for the entrance of your base

    You are allowed to have more fading doors to cover valuables in eg. a container, as long as they do not require keypad cracking.

  • 4.2
    Base designs that intentionally delay gaining entrance to the base by an excessive amount are not allowed, example: Zigzag / mazes.
  • 4.3
    Shop owners can have a fourth fading door for their enclosed buying area IF the fading door is NOT a part of the entrance.
  • 4.4
    There must be AT LEAST one entrance to your shop/base.
  • 4.5
    An entrance needs to be clearly indicated.
  • 4.6
    Do not make an entrance that requires crouching.
  • 4.7
    Your keypad granted length must be set so you have enough time to be able to get inside

    (Minimum 3 seconds)

  • 4.8
    You cannot put keypads or buttons far away from your entrance, or make them hidden

    They must be clearly visible near your door.

  • 4.9
    Do not build a base specifically made to trap people (even if it has a keypad to get out).
  • 4.10
    There must be a keypad or button linked to your fading door allowing players to enter and leave.
  • 4.11
    Using a toggled fading door does not allow you to use only one button or keypad.
  • 4.12
    You can have a maximum of one keypad or one button (each way) per fading door

    - Shop owners can have an additional button to control the fading door leading to their buying area.

  • 4.13
    Using the color/material tool to create invisible or barely visible props/buttons/cameras etc is not allowed.
  • 4.14
    Using fake keypads, buttons or a fall hatch/trap is NOT allowed.
  • 4.15
    Making or owning a base with a job of the opposite class is NOT allowed

    Example: Cops or Mayor basing with criminals.

  • 4.16
    A keypad and/or button is required on the inside of any enclosed buying area.
  • 4.17
    Do not betray players that you are basing with.
  • 4.18
    The official owner of a house is the player who owns the front door.
  • 4.19
    Do not buy random houses and doors that you aren't going to use.
  • 4.20
    Do not charge players money for houses you aren't using (You can chargenmoney if someone would like to purchase a place that you are currently using.)
  • 4.21
    Government Officials are not allowed to base with criminals or terrorists and vice versa (Being a corrupt government official is no reason to break this rule).
  • 4.22
    KOS lines/zones are NOT allowed

    The only time 'KOS' can be used is if the player has entered your property.

  • 4.23
    Hit-box abuse is not allowed

    Example: Creating a base that allows you to shoot a raider but the raider cannot shoot you back.

  • 4.24
    Fading door abuse is not allowed

    Opening a fading door using the bound key is considered fading door abuse - (Use your keypad/button!)

  • 4.25
    You must warn players to leave your base before killing them unless you have a KOS sign warning them.
  • 4.26
    Making any part of your base C4-proof or battering ram-proof is not allowed.

General Job Rules

18 rules

05
  • 5.1
    No class/job can take hostages with the exception of Osama who may take the Mayor hostage.
  • 5.2
    Do not job abuse

    Example: Dropping the weapon(s) that you spawned with as a specific job or purchasing weapons for yourself to use as another job.

  • 5.3
    Thieves and hobos ARE allowed to pickpocket afk users but may still be arrested for doing so.
  • 5.4
    You are only allowed to sell weapons as a weapon dealing job

    ( Gun dealer, BMD, Elite Seller and any custom dealer job)

  • 5.5
    The only job that can mug other players are gangsters (2 or more!)
  • 5.6
    The stun-stick and pickaxes are not considered weapons.
  • 5.7
    You are not allowed to steal anything from the miners whilst in the mine and you aren't allowed to build in it.
  • 5.8
    Ingredients and concoctions used for cooking meth/ making weed are illegal

    Equipment such as stoves, pots, jars and canisters are legal unless they're in use.

  • 5.9
    NLR does not apply if you die in an organized fight as a Fight Club Member

    You may not use weapons to fight.

  • 5.10
    Any interaction between you and any player is forgotten / forgiven once you are out of prison.
  • 5.11
    Do not randomly stun-stick players.
  • 5.12
    Only Gangsters, Cops and Terrorists can defend each other in the street.
  • 5.13
    Using cameras for other reasons than surveillance of your own base is not allowed.
  • 5.14
    By default all weapons are legal with a license, unless the laws state otherwise.
  • 5.15
    Any public homes made with props is considered to be "Inside" during a lockdown.
  • 5.16
    Advertising that you are doing something illegal is a valid reason for cops to warrant/wanted you.
  • 5.17
    WARS are not allowed (e.g

    hobos vs cops, gang A vs gang B)

  • 5.18
    As a parkour, do not place printers where other jobs can't reach.

Police

14 rules

06
  • 6.1
    You can be a corrupted officer in the sense of accepting bribes to unarrest/forget a crime

    You CANNOT accept bribes to warrant/wanted certain people and you CANNOT keep confiscated printers.

  • 6.2
    You may confiscate illegal weapons and take cash that was illegally printed.
  • 6.3
    Placing warrants or wants on people without a valid reason is NOT allowed.
  • 6.4
    You may only team up with other government officials
  • 6.5
    Weapon checking people for illegal weapons without a valid reason is NOT allowed

    You need their permission to do so.

  • 6.6
    Police must see someone commit an offence/crime to arrest, kill, warrant or want them for it. - Suspicion of illegal activity is not a valid reason!
  • 6.7
    You cannot warrant/want/arrest/kill or raid bank staff for storing illegal items.
  • 6.8
    You can still arrest Bank Guards and Managers for illegal activity such as shooting cops.
  • 6.9
    Bank Security Guards and the Bank Manager may own heavy weapons with a gun license at any time.
  • 6.10
    If you break any laws, you can be arrested or killed for it.
  • 6.11
    Police Officers and SWATs CANNOT kill prisoners if they remain in their cell

    If they somehow break the law during their prison time they may still face punishments up until their release.

  • 6.12
    You may own a house/home as a cop as long as you do your job.
  • 6.13
    You can only arrest people for breaking the law or trespassing in restricted governmental areas.
  • 6.14
    Sparks from printers are not a valid reason to warrant.

Gangsters & Mob boss

  • 9.2

  • 9.3
    Gangsters are allowed to take up unused streets that are not necessary to travel around the map for basing IF they own and use all the buildings inside the region.
  • 9.4
    When mugging players, give them at least 10 seconds to react.
  • 9.5
    You're more than welcome to take up whole apartments, if you are using it all.
  • 9.6
    Robbing/mugging people is allowed but make sure killing your victim is a last resort and make sure that the player knows you are mugging them by typing it in chat.
  • 9.7
    The maximum amount you may mug for is $1000.
  • 9.8
    Wait at least 5 minutes before mugging the same player again.
  • 9.9
    You are only allowed to steal from/rob a person with at least 2 gangsters and not with any other job.
  • 9.10
    You can only raid the PD to break a member of your gang out of jail but the Mob Boss must be with you.

Mayor

11 rules

13
  • 13.1
    Abusing laws in anyway, shape or form is NOT allowed

    Example: Making a law that every citizen needs to pay the mayor 1$ every hour.

  • 13.2
    If a crime is committed you can accept a bribe to look the other way.
  • 13.3
    You cannot make all weapons illegal

    Keep at least one weapon legal with or without a license.

  • 13.4
    You're only allowed to team up with Government officials.
  • 13.5
    Creating a lockdown without a good role-play reason is NOT allowed.
  • 13.6
    Only accept search warrants if there is a valid reason.
  • 13.7
    Do not purposely get taken hostage by terrorists.
  • 13.8
    Do not charge more than $750 for a gun license.
  • 13.9
    Do not make laws which contradict the first three default laws.
  • 13.10
    If the terrorists have taken you hostage, you do what they tell you to do (As in adding laws and placing lockdowns) but DON'T comply to actions that are against the server rules.
  • 13.11
    You may defend yourself and deny orders if they are in the act of taking you hostage but not when they have you captured inside their base.

Osama/Terrorists

11 rules
